Seek Help.
Have many assignments due today at 2359? No time to do your laundry or work?

Sometimes, getting help can be difficult when your group chats are flooded with other conversations. What happens if your closer friends are busy and you are too awkward to ask others for help directly?

Fret no more! Simply post your request with something to reward so that others will be incentivised to do the job for you!

Buy and Sell
Get your hostel supplies at ease.

Need to get a fridge for your upcoming hostel stay but new ones are simply too expensive ? From magic cleans to fans and even to portable ACs, sometimes it's hard to find buyers and sellers on Carousell. This is because the target audience is the whole of singapore, not people living in hostels! With our targeted marketplace, simply browse through your hostel supplies here!

Quick Connect
Forget Telegram or WhatsApp,
chat with your friends here in real-time.

If your group chats are often bombarded with a lot of conversations, it is going to be troublesome and tedious to facilitate a transaction there. No more toggling between apps with our in-build chat feature!

Timely Updates
Get instant updates of your progress...

We know you have many work to do as university students. It is difficult to keep track of your transactions if you are constantly flooded with assignments. Get notified instantly on your devices when there are updates on your transactions!

Get recognised.
Be the top contributor of your community.

In every community, we need to acknowledge the unsung heroes that helped our people with errands. We appreciate you and we you will definitely be recognised for your hard work. Hopefully, this will incentivise you to continue helping out in our communities!

On the Go?
From laptops to tablets and even mobile, use weShare in any of your device!

We give you cross-platform versatility. While we have previously built our UI to support desktop view, some of you have feedback that using it on mobile is more convenient.

We have listened, and we understand it is not fair to simply port over from a web-app to a native mobile app and forget about our desktop users. Instead, we have created a friendly interface that supports laptop, tablet and mobile devices!
